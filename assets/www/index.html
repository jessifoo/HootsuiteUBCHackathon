Instructions

This is a location based story game.

Each level has a series of clues that you must answer before you can proceed to the next level.

You can either try to get the clue and submit your answer or you can go to the location it tells you to go to and check in.

If it asks you to go to a coffee shop you can check into almost any coffee shop (it helps if google knows about it) and the answer will be shown.
